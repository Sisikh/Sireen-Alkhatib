ABOUT US

Our Hotel is a 5-star hotel in the heart of Beirut, offering a blend of elegance, comfort, and exceptionalservice. The hotel is dedicated to providing a warm, inviting, and personalized experience that exceeds guests' expectations. With 150 elegant rooms and suites, guests can indulge in plush furnishings, marble-clad bathrooms, flat-screen TVs,high-speed internet access, and minibars. The hotel's signature restaurant, RESTURANT, serves a variety of international dishes, including vegetarian and gluten-free options. Amenities include a 24-hour fitness center, spa services, heated outdoor pool, concierge services, business center, complimentary Wi-Fi, on-site parking, and valet service. The hotel is conveniently located in the city, near landmark attractions, major business districts,shopping centers, and entertainment venues. Our Hotel has received numerous awards and accolades for its excellence in hospitality.
