Privacy Policy

Hotel Privacy Policy

We are committed to maintaining the confidentiality and security of your personal information. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and disclose your personal information to ensure your privacy and security.

Personal Information We Collect


We collect personal information from you when you:
* Make a reservation or booking at our hotel
* Check-in or check-out at our hotel
* Provide contact information for marketing or promotional purposes
* Use our online services, such as our website or mobile app

The types of personal information we collect may include:
* Name and contact information (address, email, phone number)
* Date of birth and age
* Payment information (credit card details, etc.)
* Travel itinerary and preferences
* Special requests or needs

How We Use Your Personal Information


We use your personal information to provide you with a personalized experience at our hotel, including:
* Processing your reservation or booking
* Providing you with room assignments and key cards
* Offering you personalized services and amenities
* Communicating with you about your stay and any special requests or needs
* Conducting market research and analyzing guest behavior to improve our services

How We Disclose Your Personal Information


We may disclose your personal information to:
* Our hotel staff and management team for the purpose of providing you with services and ensuring your stay is comfortable
* Third-party service providers, such as credit card companies, airlines, and travel agencies, to facilitate your booking and travel arrangements
* Government agencies or law enforcement officials, if required by law or in response to a valid legal request
* Marketing companies and partners, to send you targeted marketing materials and offers

Security Measures


We take the security of your personal information seriously and have implemented measures to protect it from unauthorized access, disclosure, or use. These measures include:
* Encryption of sensitive data, such as credit card numbers and payment information
* Secure servers and networks to store and transmit your personal information
* Limited access to your personal information to authorized personnel only
* Regular security audits and testing to ensure our systems are secure

Your Rights

br> You have the right to:
* Request access to your personal information
* Request correction or deletion of your personal information
* Object to the use of your personal information for marketing purposes
* Withdraw consent for us to use your personal information at any time
